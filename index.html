After creating the instance you will need to have your host name (or ip address) and username ready.

First open "Putty Key Generator" and load your "private key file" that you got from your amazon account. Then "save" private key and save it as "ppk file". Then you have to have your username (for amazon it is ec2-user). then open your Putty Configuration and go to "Session" section. In the hostname field insert your hostname or ip address(for example: ec2-user@c2-52-38-157.west-2) then enter your port number number which is usually 22 then click open.

Now go to section of Connection>>SSH>>Auth and in Authentication parameter section browse your ppk file. then click open. You will see a security alert. Click "Yes" and you are now connected to your linux instance.
