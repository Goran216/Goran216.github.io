Our Team

Sofie Hafstroem - Chief Executive Officer

First member and founder of the team is Sofie from Denmark, who is our CEO and Business Development Manager for Copenhagen and Milan. She had the idea for Comeat after traveling around the world, having the best and most experiences when she knew a local or met new interesting travelers and locals. Sofie started working on Comeat in June 2017.
“I felt the memories I made with people over homemade dinners gave me so much more, that I wanted to share this joy with others … and of course so I can make use of the Comeat network myself, when I visit new cities where I don’t know anyone”
